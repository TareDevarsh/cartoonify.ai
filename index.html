Facts & Questions

Find answers to most asked questions

What is Cartoonify?

Cartoonify is an innovative online service that uses advanced AI technology, specifically Stable Diffusion models, to transform your regular photos into cartoon-like images.

How does Cartoonify work?

Our service uses a sophisticated AI model called Stable Diffusion to process and convert your uploaded photos into cartoon-style images. Simply upload your photo, and our AI does the rest!

Is Cartoonify free to use?

We offer both free and premium plans. The free plan allows you to cartoonify a limited number of images per month. For more frequent use and access to additional features, you can subscribe to our premium plan.

What types of images can I upload?

You can upload most common image formats, such as JPEG and PNG. However, to achieve the best results, we recommend using high-quality images with clear subjects.

How do you handle my privacy?

Your privacy is important to us. We only retain your images for as long as necessary to process them and do not share your personal information with third parties. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.

How can I provide feedback?

We value your feedback and would love to hear your thoughts on how we can improve our service. Please send your comments and suggestions through our Contact Us (Email Address Is not Necessary for feedback)

Can I use the cartoonified images commercially?

The use of cartoonified images for commercial purposes depends on our terms of service. Generally, images generated through our service can be used for personal and non-commercial purposes. For commercial use, please reachout to us via the Contact Us form.

Are there any restrictions on the images I can upload?

Yes, we have a few guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all users:

  • Do not upload images that contain explicit or inappropriate content.
  • Do not upload copyrighted images without permission.
How long does it take to cartoonify an image?

The processing time depends on the size and complexity of the image, but it typically takes a few seconds to a minute. You can also see your wait time in the queue.

Why is my Image disfigured?

The current tool version struggles to accurately replicate minor details due to limited server capacity.
